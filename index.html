About Us

Firebolt Kitchens is a technology first multi-brand food delivery & catering company.
We're mixing great cooking with technology to deliver food conveniently with great taste, freshness, and authenticity.
At Firebolt Kitchens we understand that good food makes happy memories, So we make sure that every order dispatched from our kitchen becomes a happy memory for our customers.

We serve on four principles-

1) Tasty & Hygienic Food
2) Customer Satisfaction
3) Super-fast Delivery
4) Eco-friendly Packaging 

As a food-tech company we aim to touch every household in India with one of our brands.
We our currently serving with 4 brands- Asian Garage, Kingdom Of Oven, Biryani Boom & Fitbite

So come join us on this amazing journey of mixing technology with food.
